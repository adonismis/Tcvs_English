E-commerce Department

I. Learning Objectives

The E-commerce Department aims to cultivate students' professional abilities in e-commerce operations, digital marketing, financial management, and financial investment. Students will learn:

  1. E-commerce platform management and operation.
  2. Digital marketing strategies, such as social media marketing.
  3. Financial management, including cost analysis and interpretation of financial statements.
  4. Fundamentals of financial investment, learning to evaluate financial products and manage risks.
  5. Data analysis and market research to cultivate business decision-making abilities.
  6. Interdisciplinary integration ability, combining knowledge of business management, marketing planning, and financial finance.

II. Learning Advantages

  1. Practice-oriented: The curriculum combines practical cases and project-based work to familiarize students with real e-commerce and financial management scenarios.
  2. Interdisciplinary learning: Covers marketing planning, business management, financial management, and financial investment to cultivate diverse skills.
  3. Industry connection: Cooperates with e-commerce companies and financial institutions to provide internship opportunities and enhance workplace competitiveness.
  4. Digitalization and financial trends: Keeps up with the latest developments in e-commerce, the digital economy, and fintech, and learns cutting-edge tools.

III. Pathways for Further Education

  1. Domestic further education: Can apply for related departments such as e-commerce, business administration, and financial finance in universities of science and technology or general universities.
  2. Study abroad: Can apply for e-commerce, digital marketing, or financial management-related programs at foreign universities.
  3. Continuing education channels: Can choose the continuing education division or in-service programs to pursue a degree while working.

IV. Career Directions

Graduates of the E-commerce Department have multifaceted skills and are suitable for a variety of employment fields:

  1. E-commerce operations: E-commerce platform management, online store operation, product listing, and sales.
  2. Digital marketing: Social media management, live-streaming influencer.
  3. Financial management: Corporate financial analysis assistant, cost control specialist.
  4. Financial investment: Financial product marketing and investment analysis assistant.
  5. Logistics management: E-commerce logistics coordination.
  6. Entrepreneurial opportunities: Start their own e-commerce brand or provide e-commerce and financial consulting services.

V. Certificate Acquisition

  1. Level B and C certificates in retail service to enhance technical competitiveness.
  2. Level B and C certificates in accounting information to enhance technical competitiveness.
  3. Level B and C certificates in computer software application to enhance technical competitiveness.
  4. Language ability: General English Proficiency Test (GEPT) to enhance international competitiveness.

Early Childhood Care and Education Department

I. Department Characteristics

The Early Childhood Care and Education Department at Toucheng Commercial and Technical Vocational High School is the only public vocational high school in eastern Taiwan with such a department. The school has an affiliated kindergarten, allowing students to learn and practice in a real environment, closely integrating theory and practice. The department has won numerous excellence awards from the county government and in childcare evaluations, and has signed cooperation agreements with industry-academia partners to provide scholarships, internships, and employment resources. Its learning outcomes are highly recognized.

II. Professional Abilities

In their three years of study, students can acquire core professional skills in early childhood care and education, including curriculum design, teaching aid production, infant and toddler care, parent education, and event performance. Through professional training and internships both on and off campus, they cultivate communication and coordination, creative thinking, and teamwork skills, laying a solid foundation for future further education and careers.

III. Future Prospects

Graduates can directly enter the education and care fields such as kindergartens, infant care centers, or after-school care centers, serving as assistant childcare providers or after-school tutors. They can also choose to pursue further education in related university departments such as early childhood education, nursing, or social work, and continue their studies to become kindergarten teachers or other education-related professionals. The diverse career paths allow students to plan their future according to their interests and expertise.

Performing Arts Department

The Performing Arts Department at Toucheng Commercial and Technical Vocational High School is the only public performing arts department in the country, dedicated to cultivating students' professional abilities and innovative performances in fields such as dance, drama, and backstage work. Students have already demonstrated numerous outstanding achievements, including being invited to perform with a Brazilian artist at the Lize Puppet Art Village, winning the championship in the national student drama competition, receiving awards in street dance competitions, and participating in major and minor events such as the Yilan New Year's Eve performance.

The Performing Arts Department provides students with many stages to showcase themselves. With professional courses and teachers, the department also guides students in obtaining a Level C cosmetology certificate, allowing them to apply their professional skills both on and off the stage in their further education and careers.
